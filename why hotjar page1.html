Workspaces

Organize your Hotjar findings so that you only see what’s relevant to you and your team.

Not every heatmap, recording, or response is relevant to you. Workspaces enable you to trim down the hours you spend digging around for insights and help you focus on what matters the most. Create a dedicated workspace for your team, a specific project, or a particular part of the user journey.
