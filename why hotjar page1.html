How it works

Tools that power your team to discover, consolidate, and communicate user needs.

Heatmaps

Heatmaps visually represent where users click, move, and scroll on your site. With this context, you'll learn how users really behave. Learn more

Recordings

Recordings are playbacks of users on your site. Watch full recordings of each visit, including the clicks, mouse movements, u-turns, and rage clicks. Identify issues on the fly and spot solutions in seconds. Learn more

Feedback

A real-time suggestion box on your site, Feedback lets users express frustration or delight about individual parts of your site, right down to the page, form, or image they're looking at. Learn more

Surveys

Surveys bring voice-of-customer to your decision-making. Gathering evidence for a new product or feature? Use a targeted Survey to validate your ideas and better understand your users. Learn more
